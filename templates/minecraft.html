The World is Yours

Minecraft is a game made of blocks that you can transform into whatever you can imagine. Play in Creative Mode with unlimited resources, or hunt for tools to fend off danger in Survival Mode.

Game Modes

Survival

Collect resources, craft items, gain levels, and manage your hunger. Watch out for Creepers!

Creative

Unlimited blocks and items. Fly around the world and build the massive castle of your dreams.

Hardcore

Survival mode, but locked to the hardest difficulty and you only have one life. Good luck.

Crafting the Future

From Redstone engineering to enchanting your diamond sword, the depth of the game is limited only by your creativity. Join millions of players in an infinite world of possibilities.
